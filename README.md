# 🎙️ VaakSakshi — Voice Legal Complaint Generator

> *VaakSakshi (వాక్సాక్షి) means "Voice Witness" in Sanskrit*

VaakSakshi converts spoken or typed grievances into complete, formal Indian legal complaint letters in under 30 seconds — in Telugu or English.

---

## The Problem

Most Indians know when their rights have been violated. A refrigerator breaks under warranty and the company refuses to replace it. A bank deducts money without authorization. A road has had a pothole for three months and nobody fixes it.

But filing a formal legal complaint requires precise legal language — correct section numbers, proper authority names, specific relief amounts, verification declarations. Most citizens cannot write this. So valid grievances go unfiled and rights go unexercised.

**VaakSakshi removes that barrier entirely.**

---

## How It Works

**Step 1 — Speak or type your problem**
Describe what happened in plain Telugu or English. No legal knowledge needed. Just say what happened, to whom, when, and how much money was involved.

**Step 2 — AI classifies your grievance**
The app automatically identifies which category your complaint belongs to — consumer, RTI, police, bank, labour, or municipal — and which specific Indian law applies to your situation.

**Step 3 — Your legal letter is generated**
A complete, formally structured legal complaint letter is produced. It is correctly addressed to the right authority, cites the exact law sections, states your relief in proper legal language, and includes a verification declaration — exactly as courts and government offices require.

**Step 4 — File it**
Download the letter as a PDF, copy the text, or share it on WhatsApp. The app also tells you exactly where to file, which portal to use, what documents to attach, and what the legal deadline is.

## Supported Complaint Types

**Consumer Complaints**
Defective products, warranty denial, service failure, e-commerce fraud, overcharging. Letters addressed to the District Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission under the Consumer Protection Act 2019.

**RTI Applications**
Requests for information from government departments. Applications addressed to the correct Public Information Officer under the Right to Information Act 2005.

**Police Complaints / FIR Requests**
Online fraud, financial cheating, criminal intimidation, cybercrime. Complaints addressed to the Station House Officer requesting FIR registration under the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita 2023.

**Banking Grievances**
Unauthorized deductions, ATM fraud, loan disputes. Letters addressed first to the Bank Nodal Officer, then escalated to the RBI Banking Ombudsman under the RBI Ombudsman Scheme 2006.

**Labour Complaints**
Salary withheld, wrongful termination, EPF violations, minimum wage violations. Complaints addressed to the Labour Commissioner under the Payment of Wages Act 1936 and the AP Shops and Establishments Act 1988.

**Municipal / Civic Grievances**
Roads, potholes, water supply, garbage, electricity. Letters addressed to the Municipal Commissioner under the AP Public Services Delivery Guarantee Act 2017.

## What Makes the Letters Legally Valid

Every letter the app generates follows the exact rules that Indian courts and government offices require:

- Addressed to the correct specific authority for the complaint amount and type
- Facts written in numbered paragraphs, each with a specific date, rupee amount, or named person
- Correct Indian law sections cited — no invented or incorrect section numbers
- All monetary amounts written in both digits and words as required
- Relief sought stated as specific rupee amounts — never vague
- Annexures listed by name — the documents the complainant must attach
- Cause of action paragraph confirming the complaint is within the legal time limit
- Verification declaration for complaints going to courts or commissions
- Correct formal closing and signature block
- AP-specific portals, helplines, and escalation paths included in filing instructions

## Language Support

The entire app UI is available in Telugu and English. Switch between them with one tap in the header.

Voice input works in Telugu (te-IN) and English (en-IN). The app understands complaints spoken or typed in either language and always generates the final legal letter in formal English, which is the required language for Indian courts and government offices.

## Andhra Pradesh Coverage

The app is built specifically for citizens of Andhra Pradesh. Filing instructions reference AP-specific portals and helplines:

- AP CM Helpline: 1902
- AP Spandana Portal for civic grievances
- AP Information Commission for RTI escalations
- AP Labour Department helpline
- MeeSeva centres for document submission
- CDMA AP for municipal administration
- AP High Court for writ petitions as last resort
